Guacomole

Guacamole Ingredients

  • Avocados
  • Fresh Garlic
  • One Lime
  • Cilantro
  • Tomatoes
  • Jalapeno
  • Onion
  • Salt

Directions

Peel and crush garlic. Dice or chop onion, avocados, garlic, cilantro, and tomatoes, then mash them up together, adding a squeeze of lime in the process.
